Seagull Restaurant - Menu

We offer a variety of seafood options, including:

  • Fish and Chips
  • Grilled Shrimp
  • Crab Cakes
  • Lobster Bisque
  • Clam Chowder

Additional Information

Discover interesting facts and trivia about our seafood dishes:

  • Fish and Chips originated in the United Kingdom and became popular during the 19th century.
  • Grilled Shrimp is a healthy and flavorful option, high in protein and low in fat.
  • Crab Cakes are typically made with lump crab meat and seasoned with herbs and spices.
  • Lobster Bisque is a creamy soup made from lobster meat, often served as an appetizer.
  • Clam Chowder is a thick soup made with clams, potatoes, onions, and cream.
